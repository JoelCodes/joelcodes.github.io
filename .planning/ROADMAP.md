# Roadmap: Joel Shinness Website

## Overview

This roadmap delivers a lead-generation focused developer portfolio for Joel Shinness targeting small business clients. The journey progresses from technical foundation through content positioning, proof points (portfolio and testimonials), conversion infrastructure (contact form and blog), and finally performance optimization. Each phase delivers verifiable capabilities that build toward the core value: small business owners can understand what Joel does, trust his process, and easily reach out.
